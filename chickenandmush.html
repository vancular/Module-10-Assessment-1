About

Chicken&Mushroom Bowls is An Indian favourite is served in flaky, puff pastry cases for a bite-sized flavour explosion. Serve as a starter or finger food at your next cocktail party

Ingredients

  • 1 sheet puff pastry, just thawed
  • 2 tsp ghee
  • 1/2 small brown onion, finely chopped
  • 62.50 ml mushroom chicken simmer sauce
  • 200g chicken thigh fillets, cut into 1cm cubes
  • 2.65 gm lemon juice
  • Coriander sprigs, to serve
